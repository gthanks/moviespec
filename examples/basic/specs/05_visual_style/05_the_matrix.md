# 05. 视觉风格：The Matrix Aesthetic

## 色彩方案 (Color Grading)
- **Tint**: `#00FF41` (Matrix Green)
- **Shadows**: Deep Blacks, High Contrast
- **Saturation**: Desaturated skin tones, heavily saturated green/cyan midtones.

## 灯光设计 (Lighting)
- **Style**: Top-down fluorescent, Chiaroscuro.
- **Atmosphere**: Cold, unnatural, mechanical.
- **Modifiers**: Haze, rim lighting to separate subjects from dark backgrounds.

## 镜头语言与材质 (Cinematography & Texture)
- **Lens**: 35mm to 50mm, slight distortion.
- **Shutter Speed**: Fast shutter for crisp action scenes.
- **Texture**: 90s film grain, sharp edges, sleek latex and leather reflections.
